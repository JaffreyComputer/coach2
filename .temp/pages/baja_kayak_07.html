Women's Baja Kayak Adventure

  • Isla Del Espiritu Santo, Mexico
  • 2007 TRIP ITINERARY (Saturday to Saturday)

MAR 3, SATURDAY, THE DAY BEFORE YOUR EXPEDITION - ARRIVAL IN LA PAZ, B.C.S.

We will welcomeyou at the airport upon your arrival in La Paz, and assist with luggage and our group transfer to the hotel. Once at the hotel you can relax, swim and explore the surroundings. Dinner and our pre-trip briefing/ Margaritas will be around 7pm. Please note: although we will dine together individuals will be responsible for their own meal and drinks on this evening.

Overnight hotel - La Paz.

 

 

MAR 4, SUNDAY, DAY 1

Breakfast will be served at the hotel between 7am-8am. Please have your expedition gear organized, packed and separated from your travel clothes (which you will be leaving behind at the hotel). Once weâ€™re all ready we will board our shuttle Van for a short transfer just north of La Paz to where our speedboat awaits. We will then board the boat for an exhilarating ride across Canal De San Lorenzo to one of two base camps on Isla Del Espiritu Santo (Holy Spirit Island). 17km long Isla Espiritu Santos western shoreline features 13 bays, idyllic sand beaches and some of the finest snorkeling and fishing in the area.

We will walk off the boat into a pre-established base camp, at which point we will have a camp orientation, lunch and a thorough orientation to safe sea kayak practices and deep-water rescues. The remainder of the day will be spent snorkeling and exploring the local waters, and/or hiking.

Meals (Continental Breakfast at hotel, lunch & dinner on island).

Overnight- Base camp 1.

MAR 5, MONDAY, DAY 2

After an early breakfast we will head off for a day of exploration. Due to the nature of the prevailing winds we will be on the water early to make the most of the day. Lunch will be served at a secluded beach and we will find ourselves back at base camp by early afternoon with plenty of time to siesta, relax, snorkel, fish and explore.

Meals - (Breakfast, lunch, dinner).

Overnight- Base Camp 1.

MAR 6, TUESDAY, DAY 3

After morning repast we will pack-up camp, in order to have everything ready to pack in the Panga (our shuttle boat). The Panga will transport the majority of the gear to the second camp, enabling us to paddle our kayaks with only the supplies we require for the days outing.

As we paddle north the seascape changes substantially. Lunch will be served at the narrow isthmus separating Isla Del Espiritu Santo from Isla la Partida, where there is an excellent snorkeling spot and sea caves nearby to explore.

We find ourselves by mid-afternoon paddling into Ensenada Grande, a large bay which is more than one mile deep. One of Ensenada's inviting sand beaches will mark the location for our second base camp. Meals (Breakfast, lunch, dinner)

Overnight base camp 2.

MAR 7, WEDNESDAY, DAY 4

A day of complete flexibility! Activities are plentiful yet optional. Perhaps an early morning hike, an afternoon of reading and snorkeling, or a night paddle. The skyâ€™s the limit and the stars in the evening sky limitless.

Meals -(Breakfast, lunch, dinner.)

Overnight - Base Camp 2.

 

MAR 8, THURSDAY, DAY 5

After breakfast, we will organize our personal gear and assist with loading the equipment boat. We will then head out on the water toward Base camp III at Punta Lobos. Lunch will be served at a beautiful beach enroute, after which we will continue our journey along the rugged east coast of the islands. We find ourselves at Punta Lobos base camp by mid-afternoon with plenty of time to freshen-up and relax before the eveningâ€™s main event â€“ dinner and antics along the Sea of Cortez.

MAR 9, FRIDAY, DAY 6

Our last morning on the islands at Punta Lobos promises to be spectacular. After breakfast there will be plenty of time to explore the wind-swept landscape, snorkel or paddle around sea stacks, while watching for the aerial antics of Bat rays as they leap from the water. We will have a fabulous island lunch at base camp, before gathering our personal gear and getting organized for the return shuttle ride across the San Lorenzo Channel. We arrive in La Paz by mid-late afternoon in time to relax, freshen up and prepare for our last evening together. We will meet for cocktails around 7pm and head out for dinner and festivities afterward.

Overnight hotel, La Paz.

MAR 10, SATURDAY. THE DAY AFTER YOUR EXPEDITION

There will be plenty of time to relax at the hotel before the return shuttle to the La Paz airport. We will plan on being at the airport approximately 2 hours prior to your flight, so please have all gear and clothing pre-packed well in advance of our departure.
