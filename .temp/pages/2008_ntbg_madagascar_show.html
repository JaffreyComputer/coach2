The trip extension to the Capelands of South Africa served as an interesting contrast to the tropical setting of Madagscar. This slideshow is courtesy of Reed and Christina Wilson who lead the NTBG Nov 2008 trip to Madgascar and So Africa. Resident NTBG scientist, David Lorence, also accompanied the group on the Madgascar portion of their tour.
