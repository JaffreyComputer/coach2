World Citizens

Terra Nova Travel believes that, as citizens of the world, we have a responsibility to protect endangered environments, species and cultures. For instance, when you travel on a safari to Southern Africa, Terra Nova Travel contributes a portion of your trip arrangement dollars to the Amy Biehl Foundation.

In keeping with this philosophy, Christina Wilson organizes a service project for every women's tour she escorts.
